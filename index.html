About Us

What is SMAW?

A Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) monitoring system is designed to track and record critical welding parameters such as current, voltage, and arc stability in real-time. This system ensures consistent weld quality, detects potential defects, and improves safety by providing immediate feedback to the welder. By analyzing the collected data, it helps in optimizing welding processes, reducing errors, and enhancing overall efficiency and productivity in welding operations.


The main motive of our project is to implement and monitor the welding equipemnts and to develop the basic welding into advanced version.
